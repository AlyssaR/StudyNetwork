StudyNetwork

The StudyNetwork gives students an opportunity to enhance their education by connecting with other students who need to study for the same classes. Through StudyNetwork students can create a profile to describe them as a student and a person. In the profile a user inputs the classes he or she are enrolled in and any organizations they are involved in. The user can then use these classes and organizations as a way to connect with similar students. User's can search for a study group by a class they are enrolled in, an organization they are a part of, or the name of the study group. The first two options give users the ability to easily find study groups that suite their needs. If a student cannot find a study group that fits what they are looking for then they can simply create a new group. The StudyNetwork is a quick and easy way for users to connect with other students and form study groups that will benefit everyone!
